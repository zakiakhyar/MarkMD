Summary: CodeMD does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All your documents and settings stay on your device.

1 Information We Collect

CodeMD is designed with privacy as a core principle. The application does not collect any personal data about you.

The following information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • A list of recently opened Markdown file URIs (stored in a local Room database)
  • App preferences such as theme, font size, font family, and other display settings (stored using Jetpack DataStore)

2 How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is nothing to use, process, or analyze. All locally stored data is used solely to provide core app functionality, specifically:

  • Displaying your recent documents on the home screen
  • Restoring your preferred display settings between sessions

3 Data Storage & Local Files

CodeMD requests storage permission only to read Markdown files you explicitly choose to open. The app does not scan, index, or access any files beyond the ones you select.

When you use the "Save to Local Storage" feature, the file is written to a location you choose via the system file picker. CodeMD does not retain a copy in any app-controlled location.

4 Internet & Network Access

CodeMD requests internet permission only for the following user-initiated actions:

  • Import from URL — When you explicitly enter a URL and tap "Open", the app fetches the content of that URL on your behalf.
  • Loading remote images — Images referenced in a Markdown document are loaded from their source URLs using Coil.

No network requests are made in the background, and no data about you is sent to any server.

5 Third-Party Services

CodeMD does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. The open-source libraries used by the app (such as Coil for image loading) operate entirely on your device and do not independently collect personal data.

6 Data Retention

All data is retained locally until you clear the app data or uninstall the app. You can also delete individual recent document entries by swiping left on them from the home screen.

7 Children's Privacy

CodeMD does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since no personal data is collected by the app at all, it is safe for use by people of any age.

8 Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be published at codemd.zaki.my.id/privacy-policy.html with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of the page.
